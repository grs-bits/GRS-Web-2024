About
Gandhi Rachnatmak Samiti

Welcome to the Gandhi Rachnatmak Samiti (GRS) at BIT Sindri! Established in 1969, GRS is a socio-cultural club dedicated to the value of public service. The club fosters a diverse and inclusive community that celebrates the talents and interests of its students. Under the guidance of Prof-In-Charge Alok Manas Dubey, GRS offers a variety of activities aimed at enhancing students' communication and social skills.
The club's focus is not only on individual development but also on building a strong sense of camaraderie and friendship among participants. GRS strives to create an environment where lasting friendships are formed, connections are made, and meaningful memories are created. By emphasizing community, GRS contributes to students' overall college experience, enabling personal and professional growth through engaging activities.
